Verdict

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro is an upcoming mid-range offering by the device maker. Accompanied by a powerful spec sheet comprising of a powerful chipset and 6GB RAM, it also offers all the desired features. Apart from featuring a mammoth capacity battery, it's powerful camera setup is sure to satisfy the buyer of the device. Additionally, the sleek body and the styling of the device adds to its list of offerings.

Elegant and Powerful

Display and Camera

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro attributes a 6.7 inches (17.02 cm) display. The AMOLED display type gets a 2.5D Curved Glass, offering a resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels with a pixel density of 393ppi to ensure high definition quality. Additionally, the device is also suspected to get a screen refresh rate of 60Hz. The camera setup of the Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro comprises of a quad-lens set-up with a primary camera of 64MP. Supporting the primary camera is an 8MP wide, ultra-wide camera and two similar 5MP lenses for wide depth photography. The other suggested features of the camera include Exposure compensation, High Dynamic Range mode (HDR), and Digital Zoom. Xiaomi has placed a 32MP shooter in the front fascia of the device for selfies and video calls.

Performance and Battery

Powering Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G chipset that rests on an octa-core processor clocking at a speed of 2.4GHz. Additionally, an Adreno 620 GPU and 6GB RAM ensures a ceaseless performance in the device. The battery capacity of the phone is at 5,100 mAh, equipped with fast charging functionality of 22.5W to ensure faster refill and extended device usage.

Storage and Connectivity

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro gets internal storage of 64GB, expandable up to 512 GB via means of MicroSD Card. The Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro runs on Android v10 paired with Xiaomi has custom UI, MIUI. The connectivity of the device offers a 5G/4G Volte network, Wi-Fi, Mobile Hotspot, Bluetooth, and A-GPS with GLONASS.
